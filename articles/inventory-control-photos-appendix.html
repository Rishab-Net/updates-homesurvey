Numbers You Can Overrule

Weight, Independent of Volume

Until now, weight and volume moved together — edit one and density math recalculated the other. Usually that’s what you want. But when you know the marble table weighs more than its cube suggests, you need to say so.

The item editor now has a Sync Weight & Volume checkbox. Leave it on and the two stay linked, recalculated by density. Turn it off and each field is yours — and the item header and AI summary show your numbers, not the recalculated ones.

The Report Upgrade

A Photos Appendix, Driven by Tags

Special-handling items deserve photographic evidence in the report — the piano that needs a crew, the credenza with the scratch that predates you. Now the survey PDF can end with a Photos Appendix: every tagged item, with its photo and its tags.

1

Configure your tags

Settings → Organization → PDF Image Tags. Choose which tags belong in the report — say, Heavy or Pre-existing Damage.

2

Tag items in review

In the dashboard, apply those tags to the items that need the paper trail.

3

Export the PDF

The report ends with a Photos Appendix: item name, photo, and applied tags for every tagged item.
